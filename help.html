Eleven Ball Help

Object of the Game:
Number on the ball indicates its value. There are 21 ball on the board plus 60 balls to be cycled into play.
The player can select any combination of balls as long as the total is equal to eleven. Repeat this process as many
time as posible but, keep an eye on the time meter. The Take Time button needs to be pressed before the Time Meter
reachs zero. If the board is cleared the round can be won as well. Depening on the difficulty selected certain amount
must be achieved to move on to the next round.
